# Empathy Map Canvas
## Project: UCab – Cab Booking Application

---

## User Persona: Regular Cab User (College Student / Working Professional)

---

### 1. WHO are we empathizing with?

| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| Name | Priya Sharma |
| Age | 23 |
| Occupation | Working Professional |
| Location | Mumbai |
| Goal | Book a cab quickly at an affordable price |

---

### 2. SAYS
*(What does the user say out loud?)*

- "I need a cab immediately but I don't know the fare beforehand"
- "I waste so much time calling different drivers to negotiate prices"
- "I wish I could see all available cabs in one place"
- "I want to know the total fare before I confirm the booking"
- "I don't feel safe booking from unknown sources"

---

### 3. THINKS
*(What is the user thinking but may not say?)*

- "Is this cab reliable and safe?"
- "Will the driver show up on time?"
- "Am I being overcharged for this ride?"
- "I wish there was a transparent pricing system"
- "I want to track my past bookings easily"

---

### 4. DOES
*(What actions does the user take?)*

- Searches for cabs on multiple platforms
- Compares prices manually
- Calls drivers directly to negotiate
- Books cabs through unofficial contacts
- Keeps manual records of trips

---

### 5. FEELS
*(What emotions does the user experience?)*

- **Frustrated** about unpredictable cab fares
- **Anxious** about safety when booking from unknown sources
- **Overwhelmed** by too many options with no easy comparison
- **Relieved** when a reliable cab is found quickly
- **Satisfied** when the fare is transparent and affordable

---

### 6. PAINS
*(Challenges and frustrations)*

- No single platform to view all available cabs
- Fare is unknown until the trip ends
- No booking history or records maintained
- Difficult to cancel a booking once made
- No role-based access — drivers and users mixed on same platform

---

### 7. GAINS
*(What does the user hope to achieve?)*

- See all available cabs in one place
- Know the fare estimate before booking
- Book, view, and cancel rides easily
- Secure and role-based login
- Reliable and fast cab booking experience

---

### 8. Empathy Map Summary

UCab addresses the core pain points by providing:
- A unified platform to browse all available cabs
- Live fare calculation (distance × price per km) before booking
- Easy booking, viewing, and cancellation of rides
- Role-based access for Users and Admins
- Secure JWT authentication
