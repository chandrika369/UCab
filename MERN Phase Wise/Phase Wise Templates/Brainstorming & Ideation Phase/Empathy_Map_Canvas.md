# Empathy Map Canvas
## Project: UCab – Cab Booking Application

---

## User Persona: Regular Cab User

| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| Name | Priya Sharma |
| Age | 23 |
| Occupation | Working Professional |
| Goal | Book a cab quickly at an affordable price |

---

## Says
- "I need a cab immediately but I don't know the fare beforehand"
- "I want to see all available cabs in one place"
- "I want to know the total fare before I confirm the booking"

## Thinks
- "Is this cab reliable and safe?"
- "Am I being overcharged for this ride?"
- "I wish there was a transparent pricing system"

## Does
- Searches for cabs on multiple platforms
- Compares prices manually
- Calls drivers directly to negotiate

## Feels
- Frustrated about unpredictable cab fares
- Anxious about safety when booking from unknown sources
- Relieved when a reliable cab is found quickly

## Pains
- No single platform to view all available cabs
- Fare is unknown until the trip ends
- No booking history or records maintained
- Difficult to cancel a booking once made

## Gains
- See all available cabs in one place
- Know the fare estimate before booking
- Book, view, and cancel rides easily
- Secure and role-based login
